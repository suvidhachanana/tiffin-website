Story of tiffin

tiffin means “food box” and this is exactly what we want you to do. The initiator of tiffin, Suvidha Khanna Chanana, was inspired by the food her mother used to cook for her as a child. She learned cooking from her mother and those very recepies are being followed with all precision and delivered to the students and everyone who wants to enjoy the authentic flavours of Indian households. We also want our customers to feel the hospitality and warmth of Indian traditional homes. Here we are welcoming you to our kitchen with folded hands in respect and love. ‘Namaste’ is the word we use to greet people which means "I bow to the divine in you"
