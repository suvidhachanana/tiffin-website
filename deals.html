Our Combo Deals

At tiffin students away from home are our target audience. We want to give them food boxes filled with memories of home and give them breathing space in their hectic schedules. We also understand the financial pressures the students feel and hence, want to contribute in our own special way. These combo deals will ensure that every student is well fed and keeping up their protein, mineral and vitamin content up-to-date.

Our Monthly Packages

Every dish at tiffin has been dedicated to the mothers in Indian kitchens and our menu’s are inspired by the foods children grow up in India, eating at home. Our menu is different for every day of the week and also provides variety of choices everyday. Our monthly packages are created to cater to you all these different flavours at lesser prices. Our monthly packages cost you $275, saving you a mighty amount of $25.
