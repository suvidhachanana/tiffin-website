What is Tiffin?

tiffin means “food box” and this is exactly what we want you to do. Our meals packaged in boxes makes the day of busy hard working people. We cater to all with love. Our food is a tribute to Indian students and local people. We try to give the taste and goodnes of home food wrapped up in tiny box to quench your hunger. We cater to authentic tastes, aromas and flavourful pallette for your taste buds.

Our Menus

Every dish at tiffin has it's own story from the traditional recipes direct from India, to modern updates that tell the history of our team, of our cooks and chefs who have left their mark on our kitchen, and our serving staff and managers are our ambassadors of hospitality. Our menu is never done - we find inspiration all around, in new ingredients, new approaches, and fresh takes on old traditional spices.
